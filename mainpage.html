BASICS OF SKIN CARE-CMP

In skincare, simplicity often reigns supreme. The essence of a good routine can be summed up in three letters: CMP. Cleanse your skin to rid it of impurities, moisturize to maintain hydration, and protect it with sunscreen against harmful UV rays. These fundamental steps form the backbone of a healthy skincare regimen, ensuring your skin stays clear, nourished, and shielded from environmental damage.
